# Game Concept

**Title:** FIAT vs CRYPTO
**Version:** v7.12.14
**Status:** Shipped (reverse-documented)

## Elevator Pitch

A vertical-scrolling shoot-'em-up where the global economy is the battlefield. Fly a currency-themed ship through waves of enemy currencies, dodge central-bank fire, and survive the crypto revolution. Every enemy is a currency symbol, bosses are central banks, and the economy itself is both the theme and the difficulty mechanic.

## Core Fantasy

The player is a currency trader turned pilot, blasting through financial markets gone rogue. What if the economy was a shoot-em-up? Inflation makes enemies tougher. Bear markets spawn more of them. The GODCHAIN crypto event throws the whole market into chaos. The player doesn't just fight enemies — they fight macroeconomics.

## Target Platform

- Web (PWA) — desktop and mobile browsers
- Canvas 2D + vanilla JavaScript, no framework
- 60fps target with 200+ concurrent entities
- Offline-capable via service worker

## Game Pillars

1. **Economic Satire Through Gameplay** — Every system reinforces the core joke. Enemies as currency symbols, bosses as central banks, Bear Market as difficulty mechanic. The satire is embedded in mechanics, not skins.

2. **Juicy, Responsive Combat** — Screen shake, hit-stop, glow passes, particle bursts, impact flash — every shot and hit is physically satisfying.

3. **Risk-Reward Decision Density** — DIP meter for close-range trading, HYPER mode timing, modifier cards with tradeoffs. The best play is rarely the safest.

4. **Two Distinct Modes, One Core Loop** — V8 Campaign (curated, scripted) and Arcade Mode (roguelike, procedural, infinite). Same combat core, different pacing systems.

5. **PWA-First, Zero-Friction Access** — No download, no account, no login. Works offline. Mobile-friendly.

## Visual Identity Anchor (Progression)

**A journey from Earth's blue sky to the neon void.** The game begins in low Earth orbit with a natural, realistic palette — blue gradient sky, white clouds, green hills, daylight horizon glow. As the player ascends through the campaign, the sky gradually shifts from blue to violet (Phase 2), clouds thin and stylize, and stars begin to emerge. By Phase 3 (deep space), the background is pure black with the full cyberpunk neon aesthetic — violet, cyan, green, magenta accents against the void. This is a Bloomberg Terminal crossed with a 1980s arcade cabinet, but you have to earn it: the journey from terrestrial daylight to cosmic ledger is itself the visual arc. Currency symbols are rendered as geometric ship shapes throughout. UI uses monospace typography with glow effects, glass-panel overlays, and terminal-style borders, maintaining visual consistency across all three phases.
