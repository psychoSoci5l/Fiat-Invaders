# Game Pillars

**Last Updated**: 2026-04-25

---

### 1. Economic Satire Through Gameplay

Every system — enemies as currency symbols, bosses as central banks, GODCHAIN as crypto mania, Bear Market as difficulty mechanic — reinforces the core joke: *what if the economy was a shoot-em-up?* The satire is embedded in mechanics, not just skins. Players *feel* inflation through enemy HP scaling, *feel* the bear market through weather and rank shifts.

### 2. Juicy, Responsive Combat

Screen shake, hit-stop, additive glow passes, particle bursts, impact flash, combo HUD pulse, floating damage numbers — every shot and hit is physically satisfying. The combat feel must never be sacrificed for visual complexity. Target: 60fps with 200+ entities, snappy hit feedback at any entity count.

### 3. Risk-Reward Decision Density

The player constantly chooses: go closer for DIP meter gain? Activate HYPER now or save it? Take a risky modifier card for a bigger multiplier? Push through the combo timer or dodge? The best play is rarely the safest play. Systems should create interesting tradeoffs, not obvious optimal paths.

### 4. Two Distinct Modes, One Core Loop

V8 Campaign delivers a curated, scripted shoot-em-up experience (Gradius-style level design with boss set-pieces). Arcade Mode delivers roguelike replayability with procedural waves, modifier stacking, and infinite scaling. Both modes share the same combat core — the player, weapons, enemies, drops — but are gated by separate systems (LevelScript vs WaveManager). Neither mode should feel like a lesser version of the other.

### 5. PWA-First, Zero-Friction Access

Launch in browser, no download, no account, no login. The game loads on any device with a modern browser and works offline after first visit. Mobile-friendly canvas scaling, touch-adjacent controls (mouse), and standalone PWA mode. Every barrier removed between thought and play.
